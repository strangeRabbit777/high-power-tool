## The `remove-me` Extension

The `remove-me` extension allows you to remove an element after a specified interval.

### Usage

```html
<div hx-ext="remove-me">
    <!-- Removes this div after 1 second -->
    <div remove-me="1s">To Be Removed...</div>
</div> 
```
